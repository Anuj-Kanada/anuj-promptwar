⚡ Quick Stress Busters

🧊

Ice Cube Hold

Hold an ice cube for 60 seconds. The cold sensation grounds you in the present moment.

1 min
💪

Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Tense each muscle group for 5 seconds, then release. Start from toes to head.

5 min
🎵

Humming Bee Breath

Close your eyes, breathe in deeply, hum as you exhale. Repeat 5 times. The vibration calms your nervous system.

3 min
📝

Worry Dump

Write down everything worrying you for 3 minutes. Don't filter, just write. Then close the paper.

3 min
🚶

Mindful Walk

Take a 5-minute walk. Focus on each step, the feeling of ground beneath your feet.

5 min
🙏

Gratitude Flash

Name 3 things you're grateful for right now. They can be tiny. This shifts your brain from threat to safety mode.

1 min
